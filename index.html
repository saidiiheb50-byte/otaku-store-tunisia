About Hadrumet Store

Welcome to Hadrumet Store, your premier destination for authentic manga merchandise in Tunisia. We specialize in bringing you the best collection of manga stickers, books, and posters from your favorite series.

Our Mission

We are passionate about anime and manga culture and committed to providing high-quality products to fellow otakus across Tunisia. Our goal is to make authentic manga merchandise accessible to everyone.

Shipping Policy

We offer delivery throughout Tunisia:

  • Home Delivery: 5 TND - Delivered to your door within 3-5 business days
  • Store Pickup: Free - Pick up your order at our store location

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a confirmation email or WhatsApp message once your order is ready.

Payment

We accept cash on delivery and bank transfers. Payment details will be confirmed when you place your order.
